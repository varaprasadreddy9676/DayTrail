# Code of Conduct

DayTrail contributors are expected to keep discussions respectful, direct, and focused on improving the project.

## Expected Behavior

- Be constructive and specific.
- Respect different experience levels and operating environments.
- Treat security and privacy reports carefully.
- Avoid personal attacks, harassment, and discriminatory language.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or abuse.
- Publishing private information or captured user data.
- Publicly disclosing exploitable vulnerabilities before maintainers have had time to respond.
- Repeated disruptive behavior after being asked to stop.

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or restrict participation to protect the project and its users.
